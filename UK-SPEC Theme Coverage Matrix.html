Key observation: Sustainability, safety, and ethics each appear explicitly in just one section (E). Resilience, systems thinking, innovation, and human focus are absent from the competence wording entirely. Several themes present in EngTech/ICTTech B (safety, security, environmental impact) disappear at IEng and CEng level. Workshop participants consistently called for cross-cutting themes to be “threaded” across all sections, not siloed or buried.
